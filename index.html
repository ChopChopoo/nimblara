Nimblara

Built for the long trail.

Nimblara is a small trail-running brand based in Bend, Oregon, founded in 2023. The line is two trail shoes built around the GripCore outsole, a 4 mm lug pattern designed for mixed terrain (packed dirt, loose gravel, wet roots) rather than the extremes of either pure mountain or pure groomed trail. The shoes are aimed at ultra-distance runners and weekend trail competitors who want a single shoe that handles the variety of a typical 50K course. Nimblara also runs a small repair program: send back a worn pair and we will resole them for a fraction of new-shoe cost.
