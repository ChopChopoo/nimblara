What runners are saying

Selected reviews from Nimblara customers and independent running clubs. We publish the full list of submitted reviews on request.

“Did the Cascade 50K in the Ridge last September. The GripCore outsole was the difference on the wet descent at mile 28 — my training partner in a competing shoe slipped twice on the same section where I had no issues.”

— Samira O., Bend, OR

“Ordered the Ridge LT for shorter weekend trail runs. Lighter than I expected for the stack height. The lacing system holds the midfoot well without pressure points, even on technical descents.”

— Theo R., Boulder, CO
